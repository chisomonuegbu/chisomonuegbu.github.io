I'm Chisom — an engineer who spends most of his time on infrastructure, DevOps, and a homelab that has grown more serious than it was supposed to.

This site is where I write the work down. Posts fall into three buckets:

- **Deep dives** — long-form writeups when I've finished something substantial and there's a story worth telling.
- **Notes** — short posts about a small thing I learned and want to find again later.
- **Postmortems** — incidents and failures, written so I remember why.

Most of the writing is for whoever finds it useful, and partly for me. Writing things down is the closest thing I have to actually understanding them.

The best way to reach me is on [GitHub](https://github.com/chisomonuegbu) or by [email](mailto:chisom@chisomonuegbu.dev). I'm slow to reply but I do reply.
